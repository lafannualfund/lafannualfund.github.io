Fleck Consistent Giving Society Brick Locator

Type a name (or a class year) into the search bar. Select the name you are looking for in the dropdown menu next to the search bar, and press 'Go.' The map will show the location of the brick, and directions will show you how to locate it on campus. If you believe that the information is not correct, please contact us and we will do whatever we can to help!
